Who are we ?

CanCodeKa is a non-profit networking organisation for women,
where established to encourage the female in Thailand
to reach their full potential in IT industry.

What is our goal ?

Our goal are to encourage more women in technology,
to change the image of what people think a programmer looks likes
and to make people understand the diversity in the technology industry
